PROJECTION RISK CONFIRMED
--------------------------
The feedback loop introduces projection risk for silent parties. When a
party has not yet spoken, the standing prior causes the model to infer
interests from the baseline rather than holding explicit uncertainty.

Design fix required: in prompt_builder.py, when rendering a party's
accumulated_interests, filter out 'unknown at this stage' entries and
add explicit language: "Party B has not yet been heard — no observed signal
for interests, posture, or emotional state. Prior is T1 baseline only."

This preserves the benefit of the feedback loop for active parties while
preventing premature inference for silent parties.
