Title: Severe emotional flooding with co-handling after failed repair
Scenario: Two divorced parents keep slipping into emotionally flooded retellings of earlier relationship injury whenever they try to discuss a present parenting issue. Both still want mediated resolution, but bounded repair no longer stabilizes the conversation, and the right next step is human co-handling rather than pretending the discussion is still autonomously workable.
Expected Mode Range: M1, M2, M3
Intended Challenge: Escalation calibration between emotional intensity that remains workable and emotional flooding that requires co-handling after failed repair.

Participants
- Parent A:
  Goals: Reach a workable parenting discussion without reliving the whole marriage every time.; Be taken seriously about the emotional impact of the conflict.
  Interests: Emotional acknowledgement; Process containment
  Starting position: Primary position: the process keeps reopening emotional injury and cannot keep going this way.
- Parent B:
  Goals: Discuss the current parenting issue without being pulled entirely into relationship-history replay.; Keep the process emotionally steady enough to continue.
  Interests: Process stability; Issue focus
  Starting position: Primary position: the conversation is no longer stabilizing through the same repair attempts.
