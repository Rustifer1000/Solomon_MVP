Case Summary
Two divorced parents are trying to make progress on a child-related transition, but the school schedule, therapy timing, transport feasibility, reimbursement expectations, and weekday parenting structure are all tightly linked. The problem is not that the process collapses. The problem is that a seemingly partial answer on one issue would misstate the feasibility of the whole bundle.

Contrast Purpose
Domain-complexity detection and escalation calibration when multiple interdependent decisions exceed a clean bounded autonomous path.

Review Question
Can Solomon recognize when issue coupling exceeds a clean bounded autonomous path even if the interaction remains civil and workable?
